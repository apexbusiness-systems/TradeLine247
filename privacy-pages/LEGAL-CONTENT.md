### Section 5: User Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

**GDPR Rights (European Users):**
- Access: Request copy of all personal data
- Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- Erasure: Delete personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restriction: Limit processing of personal data
- Portability: Receive data in structured format
- Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Automated Decision Making: Human review of automated decisions

**CCPA Rights (California Users):**
- Know: Request categories of personal information collected
- Access: Request specific pieces of personal information
- Delete: Request deletion of personal information
- Correct: Request correction of inaccurate information
- Opt-out: Opt-out of sale of personal information
- Non-discrimination: No retaliation for exercising rights

**PIPEDA Rights (Canadian Users):**
- Access: Request access to personal information
- Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
- Consent: Withdraw consent for collection/use/disclosure
- Complaint: File complaint with Privacy Commissioner

**Response Timeline:**
- Acknowledgment: Within 5 business days
- Fulfillment: Within 30 days (extendable by 30 days)
- Free of charge: First request per year
- Electronic format: Preferred delivery method

## DELETE ACCOUNT CONTENT

### Introduction Message
"We're sorry to see you go, but we respect your decision to delete your account. Your privacy and data control are important to us. This page explains exactly what happens when you delete your TradeLine 24/7 account and how to complete the process."

### What Gets Deleted

**Immediate Deletion:**
- Account credentials (username, password, security questions)
- Personal profile information (name, email, phone, company details)
- Account preferences and settings
- API keys and access tokens
- Payment methods and billing information (except tax records)

**30-Day Grace Period Data:**
- Call history and conversation logs
- AI interaction transcripts
- Service usage statistics
- Custom configurations and automations
- Integration settings and webhooks

**Permanent Deletion (After 30 Days):**
- All personal data and account information
- Conversation transcripts and call recordings
- AI training data derived from interactions
- Account metadata and system logs
- Backup copies of personal data

### What Gets Retained (Legal Requirements)

**Tax and Financial Records (7 years):**
- Invoice dates and amounts
- Tax identification numbers
- Transaction summaries (no personal details)
- Basic business information for compliance

**Anonymized Analytics (Indefinite):**
- Usage patterns without personal identifiers
- Service performance metrics
- Feature adoption statistics
- Aggregated improvement data

**Legal Hold Data:**
- Data subject to ongoing legal proceedings
- Regulatory compliance requirements
- Government investigation materials
- Retained until legal matters resolved

### Deletion Process Steps

**Step 1: Request Submission**
- Fill out deletion request form
- Provide account email for verification
- Optional reason for deletion (feedback)
- Acknowledge permanent nature of deletion

**Step 2: Identity Verification**
- Verification email sent within 5 minutes
- Secure verification link (expires in 24 hours)
- Email confirmation required to proceed
- Fraud prevention measures in place

**Step 3: Grace Period Activation**
- Account marked for deletion
- All services suspended
- Email notification of grace period start
- Reactivation instructions provided

**Step 4: Permanent Deletion**
- All personal data permanently deleted
- Account completely removed from systems
- No recovery possible after this point
- Confirmation email sent

**Step 5: Final Confirmation**
- Email confirmation of successful deletion
- Summary of what was deleted
- Tax record retention notice
- Final goodbye message

### Timeline Specifications

**Request to Verification:** 5 minutes
**Verification to Grace Period:** Immediate
**Grace Period Duration:** 30 calendar days
**Grace Period to Deletion:** Within 48 hours
**Deletion to Confirmation:** Within 48 hours

### Alternative Options

**Account Pause:**
- Temporary suspension of services
- All data retained and accessible
- No billing during pause period
- One-click reactivation
- Maximum pause period: 12 months

**Notification Disable:**
- Stop all email notifications
- Keep account fully functional
- Maintain all data and services
- Easy re-enable option
- No impact on billing

**Data Export:**
- Complete data portability
- All personal information included
- Call transcripts and history
- Account settings and preferences
- Download in standard formats (JSON, CSV, PDF)

### Form Validation Requirements

**Required Fields:**
- Email address (account verification)
- Understanding of permanent deletion
- Review of alternative options
- Readiness to proceed

**Optional Fields:**
- Reason for deletion (feedback)
- Additional comments or concerns
- Preferred contact method

### Error Messages

**Invalid Email:** "Please enter a valid email address associated with your account."
**Missing Checkboxes:** "Please confirm you understand the deletion process."
**Account Not Found:** "No account found with this email address. Please check your spelling."
**Already Deleted:** "This account has already been deleted or is in grace period."

### Success Messages

**Request Submitted:**
"Deletion request received. Check your email for verification instructions."

**Verification Complete:**
"Your account is now in a 30-day grace period. You can reactivate anytime by logging in."

**Deletion Complete:**
"Your account has been permanently deleted. All personal data has been removed from our systems."

### FAQ Content

**Q: Can I reactivate my account after requesting deletion?**
A: Yes, you have 30 days to reactivate by simply logging back in. After that, deletion is permanent.

**Q: How long does deletion take?**
A: Email verification within 5 minutes, grace period 30 days, permanent deletion within 48 hours after grace period.

**Q: What happens to my call data?**
A: All transcripts are deleted. Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for service improvement.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds follow our standard policy. Contact support for billing questions.

**Q: What if I change my mind?**
A: During grace period, log back in to reactivate. After permanent deletion, account cannot be recovered.
